# Ghost Mannequin Catalog Standard v1

This document defines the immutable visual rules for all product images.
All generation, editing, and post-processing MUST conform.

---

## 1. Camera & Framing (LOCKED)

- View: Straight-on frontal view
- Camera height: Centered at product midpoint
- Perspective: Neutral (no wide-angle distortion)
- Rotation: 0° (no tilt, no perspective skew)

---

## 2. Canvas & Crop (LOCKED)

- Aspect ratio: 1:1
- Output resolution: 2048 × 2048 minimum
- Product occupies ~72–78% of frame width
- Equal padding left/right
- Slightly more breathing room above than below

---

## 3. Product Positioning (LOCKED)

- Product centered horizontally
- Bottom edge visually grounded (soft shadow allowed)
- No floating unless explicitly specified per product class

---

## 4. Background (LOCKED)

- Primary background: Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- Acceptable neutral fallback: #F4F4F4
- No gradients
- No textures
- No environment context

---

## 5. Lighting (LOCKED)

- Soft studio lighting
- Even illumination
- No dramatic shadows
- No hard highlights
- Subtle grounding shadow only

---

## 6. Materials & Accuracy (CRITICAL)

- Preserve exact material texture
- Preserve seams, stitching, hardware placement
- No added branding
- No removed elements
- No reshaping or “beautification”

Accuracy > aesthetics.

---

## 7. Ghost Mannequin Rules

- No visible human features
- No visible mannequin forms
- Internal volume implied only by shape
- Natural tension preserved in straps/handles

---

## 8. Disallowed (NEVER)

- Model hallucination
- Added straps, tags, or hardware
- Symmetry correction unless present in original
- Background color drift
- Perspective change

---

## 9. Post-Processing Allowances

Allowed:
- Background cleanup
- Minor centering
- Exposure normalization

Not allowed:
- Shape correction
- Texture smoothing
- AI “enhancement”

---

## 10. Consistency Over Time

Any future update must:
- Be versioned
- Never break historical images
